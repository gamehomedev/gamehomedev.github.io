PongGame Privacy Policy

Effective date: 25.06.2026

PongGame is a casual arcade game where you control a paddle and bounce a ball through a wall gap. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information.

Information we collect

PongGame does not require an account, sign-in, camera access, microphone access, contacts, user media access, or precise location access. We do not ask users to directly provide personal information to play the game.

The app uses Google Mobile Ads / AdMob to show ads. Because ads are provided by Google, Google may collect or process information such as advertising identifiers, device information, app activity related to ads, ad interactions, diagnostics, crash or performance data, IP address, and approximate location derived from network or device signals. This processing depends on Google's SDKs, user settings, consent choices where required, and ad configuration.

Gameplay data

The game uses temporary gameplay state, such as timer, score, paddle position, and ball position, to run the game. This information is used only for app functionality and is not sent to developer servers.

Ads

PongGame may display banner ads and rewarded interstitial ads through Google Mobile Ads / AdMob. The app declares the Android Advertising ID permission so Google Mobile Ads can support advertising, fraud prevention, frequency capping, reporting, and ad measurement where available.

Ads may be personalized or non-personalized depending on Google settings, regional consent requirements, and the app's final release configuration. You can learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use its services at https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites, and Google's Privacy Policy is available at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Analytics and diagnostics

If Firebase or Firebase Analytics is enabled in a future release, it may be used to understand app usage, app performance, crashes, and diagnostics. Firebase may process data such as app interactions, device information, identifiers, diagnostics, and approximate location according to Google's policies and SDK configuration.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information processed by Google Mobile Ads / AdMob, and Firebase if enabled, may be shared with Google and its partners for advertising, analytics, diagnostics, fraud prevention, security, and app functionality according to Google's policies.

User choices

Users can manage ad personalization and the Android Advertising ID through their device settings and Google account settings. Users can also uninstall the app or clear app storage to remove local app data.

Children's privacy

The app is intended for a general audience and is not specifically directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. If the app is later configured for child-directed audiences or families, ads and data handling must be configured to comply with the applicable Google Play and Google advertising requirements.

Data deletion

Because the app does not create accounts, there is no account data to delete. Users can remove local app data by clearing the app storage or uninstalling the app. Data processed by Google services is handled according to Google's policies and available user controls.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes. Any updates should be posted at the hosted privacy policy URL used in Google Play Console.

Contact
